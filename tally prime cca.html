TALLY PRIME CCA

(Certified Computer Accountant)




Objective

Upon completion of this course, the candidate will get exposed to all features of Tally Prime and be able to cater to the financial needs of any business organization.

Target Audience

B.Com, M.Com, BCA, BCS, ICWA, CA, ACS, CRA, MBA Accounting professionals, and +2 students.

Prerequisites

Basic knowledge of accounting.

Overview

This course extensively covers Tally Prime with live examples based on industrial verticals.
